About

I am a PhD candidate (2022–present) in Earth and Planetary Sciences at Brown University. My research interests focus on (1) the energetics of the Earth–Moon system and (2) how micro- to nanoscale processes dictate geological phenomena.

Research

  • Lunar thermal evolution — magma ocean solidification, tidal heating, and hemispheric asymmetry.
  • Micro-/nano-analysis — SIMS U–Pb, APT, and trace-element characterization of planetary materials.
